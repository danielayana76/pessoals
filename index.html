Where Personal Brands Become Icons.

Our Process ( ICON Method )

Step 1:

Identification

It all starts with you. In a quick but powerful 15-minute discovery call, we dive into who you are, what you stand for, and where you’re headed. We uncover the essence of your story, values, and expectations setting the foundation for a personal brand that’s true to you.

Step 2:

Creative Direction

Next, we transform insights from the Identification phase into a tailored personal brand strategy. This includes your positioning, differentiation, tone of voice, and more. We also craft 3 unique moodboards for creative direction each capturing a different vibe. You’ll pick the one that feels like home.

Step 3:

Own it

Here’s where we make it yours. Based on the mood you select, we design a complete personal brand identity. You get 3 logo concepts, a color palette, and key visual elements that express your unique style ready to make your mark.

Step 4:

Nurture

Now it’s time to grow. We deliver a content strategy and social media content plan built for consistency and impact. Plus, we design custom content templates to help you stay on-brand effortlessly so your personal brand keeps thriving.
